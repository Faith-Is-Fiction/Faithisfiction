Automated Railway Crossing Safety Model Using Laser Sensing

late 2023

In 2023, during my time in Grade 7, I participated in a science exhibition where I presented an innovative solution designed to enhance safety at railway-road junctions. My project aimed to automate the traditional manual operation performed by personnel stationed at these crossings. The model utilized a laser sensor system to detect approaching trains. When a train interrupted the laser beam, the system would automatically block the railway crossing and activate a warning buzzer, alerting pedestrians and vehicles to stop. This smart mechanism significantly reduces human effort and enhances safety by providing timely alerts and automatic gate control.
